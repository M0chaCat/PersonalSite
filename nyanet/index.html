Welcome to the open internet made for kitties

Nyanet is a cozy, custom DNS-powered web where sites live on many custom TLDs like .paw, .kitten, .root, and even stuff like .pineapple!

It’s not on the "real" web — it’s on our web.

  • - fwee for anyone domains

  • - No ads, no trackers, just chill
  • - works with any device via custom DNS!

How to join

  • 1. Add 209.25.143.66 to your DNS — above your current ones (like 209.25.143.66, 1.1.1.1)
  • 2. Visit a Nyanet site like home.root!
  • 3. Meow around and make the web ours :3
